Why I chose programming:

Funny story - my very first interest in programming started by working on a very small project... for my wife's business class in late 2017. She isn't a huge techie person, so she asked me to help. The project she had to do for her class was to build a resume, but in an HTML format. She was frustrated and didn't understand anything that was happening, so with my tech background and love for [anything] technology, I jumped in and assisted her in making the HTML document. Without any prior knowledge in coding, I managed to finish a VERY basic HTML document that had her basic resume information on it in ~3 hours.

It was then that I decided to switch from being a potential PA student to a coding novice (hope to be a master soon!). My biggest interest is back-end language and server side development, but I enjoy learning the full spectrum. I feel like it never hurts knowing a little about everything!
